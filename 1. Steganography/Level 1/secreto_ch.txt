The city of Chihuahua
is the state capital 
of the Mexican state of Chihuahua.
As of 2015, the city of Chihuahua 
had a population of 878,062 inhabitants
while the metropolitan area had a 
population of 918,339 inhabitants.
A substantial population is from the U.S.
